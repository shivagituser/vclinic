Welcome to V-Clinic

our staff, faculty, students, volunteers and supporters are dedicated to improving the lives of people and families affected by psychiatric illness. As the largest psychiatric affiliate of Harvard Medical School, McLean has been meeting the needs of individuals and families with psychiatric illness since our founding in 1990. We are here to help you.

Our Solution

Child Life Services

The Child Life staff helps normalize the hospital environment, providing psychosocial support and educational programs for infants, children, adolescents, and their families in order to minimize the stress and anxiety related to hospitalization.

Emergency & Urgent Care

When a sudden serious or life-threatening medical emergency strikes, it’s important to know you have close-to-home access to around-the-clock, expert care in an environment specially designed for infants and children.

Gastroenterology - Nutrition

With five board-certified, fellowship-trained pediatric gastroenterologists, V-Clinic Hospital offers Maine’s largest and most comprehensive resource for managing digestive, liver and nutritional disorders in infants, children, adolescents and young adults.

Cardiology & Cardiac Surgery

Our pediatric cardiology team is made up of five board-certified and fellowship-trained subspecialists, a board-certified, fellowship-trained pediatric cardiac surgeon – the only one in Maine, and a nurse practitioner. Together, they treat Patients with virtually every kind of heart condition, from the most routine to the most complex.
